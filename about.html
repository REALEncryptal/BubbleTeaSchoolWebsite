History

The history of Bubble Tea, also known as Boba Tea (BBT), can be traced back to Taiwan in the 1980s. It was created by mixing sweet milk tea with chewy tapioca pearls, and over the years, it has evolved into a global phenomenon with a wide variety of flavors and toppings.
